Incandescence, the annual cultural festival of NIT Silchar emerges to be the most anticipated event not only in the whole of North East but also across the country. Incandescence stands apart for being the most vibrant, enthusiastic and energetic event in the Northeast. The students maintain the culture of delivering dedication and perfection up to the highest level. From eminent poets from across the country conveying values with melodious verses in “Kavi Sammelan” to wild and energetic performance in “Thundermarch” Incandescence has had it all over the years. It has evolved as the motivator for many with “Carpe diem” the star night. Competitiveness in a healthy manner is always encouraged as Incandescence also provides platform to prove one’s talent in dance, music and many more. As a youth festival we revere our responsibility towards the society and keep the mettle to fulfill it. Street Plays, Blood Donation camps, helping poor children though Gyansagar and steering the younger generation through “Razzmatazz”, we NITSians never fall behind when it comes to humanity.

Thundermarch is the annual rock and metal fest of National Institute of Technology Silchar.[1] It is an integral part of Incandescence, the annual cultural festival of NIT Silchar, held in the month of February, and is one of the most popular and widely attended rock festivals in North-East India.

History

Thundermarch is the flagship event of Incandescenece, NIT Silchar the cultural festival of NIT Silchar, which is a non-profit organisation run completely by the students under the guidance of the Gymkhana Union Body,the student body of NIT Silchar.

It started as simple rock festival in the year 2009 and has now grown into one of the largest rock festivals in the country and a platform for the national bands to battle for the best. The fest is regularly headlined by International acts and the best bands in the country.
